WHO IS JEFF?

A Data Analyst and Sr. Manager with 8+ years of experience, passionate about solving problems and building lasting solutions. The journey started with a paper route and washing dishes, experiences that instilled a tenacious work ethic that carried through managing commercial kitchens and into a long, successful career at Verizon.

At Verizon, I led data projects from engineering to visualization, transforming complex information into clear business intelligence. My work has empowered over 10,000 users through 300+ dashboards, generating more than 2 million views and fostering a data-driven culture.

As a Salt Lake City native, I have a deep love for outdoor recreation and finding new adventures in the mountains, whether it's hiking, camping, or finding a new UTV trail.
